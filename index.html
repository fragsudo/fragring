welcome to the fragcord webpage (fragweb)!

what is the fragweb? the fragweb is this webpage dedicated for showcasing the perfume/fragrance blogs of the fragcord members. you can click on any badge to go to their respective blogs!

what is a webring? what's the fragring? a webring is a collection or group of websites connected to each other via a circular "ring"-like structure. typically they are used to link together blogs who post about the same topics. the fragring is a webring that some of the blogs below are part of. you can easily traverse the fragring by going to any blog below that is part of it via the "previous" and "next" arrows!

if you would like to join either the fragweb and/or the fragring, please follow the instructions listed here!
